About Me

Hello, I'm Rethabile Makaotsi, born on the 28th of February, 2001. I live in Khayelitsha, a vibrant community in Cape Town, South Africa. My journey into web development began in 2023 when I joined the Life Choice Coding Academy bootcamp, where I gained a solid foundation in programming and development frameworks.

As a web developer, I create responsive and visually appealing websites and ensure seamless integration and functionality with back-end technologies. When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new places, reading, and spending time with family and friends.
